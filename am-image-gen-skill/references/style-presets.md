## 🏷️ Logo

### L1: Geometric Minimal
- Clean geometric shapes, flat colors, bold sans-serif typography
- White/light background, single accent color
- Best for: tech, SaaS, modern brands

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (L1)</summary>

A modern minimalist logo for a tech company called "NEXAFLOW". Clean geometric lettermark using the letters "N" and "F" interlocking, formed from simple angular shapes. Bold sans-serif typography below reading "NEXAFLOW" in medium weight. Single accent color: electric blue (#2563EB) on pure white background. Flat design, no gradients, no shadows. The icon should work at small sizes (favicon). Vector-style rendering, crisp edges, professional corporate identity, Silicon Valley aesthetic. No text other than the brand name.
</details>

### L2: Luxury Emblem ✨
- Metallic gold/silver on dark background, serif typography
- Crest/shield/monogram motifs, fine line details
- Best for: fashion, jewelry, premium F&B, hospitality
- Visual cue: _"Nghĩ kiểu Versace, Cartier — gold emblem trên nền đen"_

### L3: Playful Mascot 🐻
- Cartoon character/animal, rounded shapes, vibrant colors
- Friendly, approachable, memorable silhouette
- Best for: F&B, kids, gaming, community brands
- Visual cue: _"Kiểu Duolingo, Shopee — mascot dễ thương dễ nhớ"_

### L4: Organic Handcraft 🌿
- Hand-drawn lettermarks, natural textures, earth tones
- Watercolor/stamp/woodcut aesthetics
- Best for: artisan food, eco brands, local shops
- Visual cue: _"Kiểu tiệm cà phê indie, sản phẩm organic handmade"_

### L5: Bold Wordmark 🔤
- Typography-only, custom lettering, strong weight contrast
- Minimal decoration, color = identity
- Best for: media, agency, personal brand
- Visual cue: _"Kiểu Supreme, FedEx — chỉ có chữ nhưng đậm chất"_

## 🖼️ Banner / Ad Creative

### B1: Product Hero
- Product centered, dynamic angle, splash/particle effects
- Gradient background matching brand color, bold headline
- Best for: product launch, sale announcement

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (B1)</summary>

A vibrant product launch banner for a new citrus energy drink called "ZESTBOLT". Square composition (1:1) for Instagram. The aluminum can is centered at a dynamic 15-degree tilt, covered in cold condensation droplets, glowing golden-orange liquid splashing dramatically from the top. Background is a radial gradient from deep orange (#EA580C) at edges to bright yellow (#FACC15) at center. Scattered ice cubes and citrus slices (2 orange wedges, 1 lime half) in the foreground. Bold sans-serif headline at top reading "NEW FLAVOR DROP" in white with subtle drop shadow. Smaller text at bottom reading "ZERO SUGAR • MAXIMUM ENERGY" in white caps. Glossy high-energy studio lighting with strong backlight creating rim highlights on the can. Crisp packaging detail, realistic droplets, polished commercial ad photography. No watermark.
</details>

### B2: Lifestyle Context
- Product in-use within aspirational scene (café, gym, beach, office)
- Soft natural lighting, editorial feel, subtle text overlay
- Best for: brand awareness, storytelling campaigns

### B3: Split Layout
- Left side: bold text/offer. Right side: product/lifestyle image
- Clean divider, high contrast sections
- Best for: promotional ads with clear CTA

### B4: Festive/Seasonal
- Holiday-specific elements (flowers, snowflakes, lanterns...)
- Warm/celebratory colors, decorative frames
- Best for: seasonal campaigns (Tết, Christmas, Black Friday)

### B5: Comparison/Before-After
- Side-by-side or sequential frames showing transformation
- Arrows, labels, clear visual difference
- Best for: skincare, fitness, cleaning products

### B6: Flat Illustration
- Custom vector-style illustration, brand characters
- Geometric shapes, limited palette (3-4 colors max)
- Best for: tech, app promotion, educational content

## 📦 Product Photography (E-commerce)

### P1: Clean Studio
- Pure white or light gradient background
- Product centered, multiple angles possible
- Soft even lighting, no shadows or minimal contact shadow
- Best for: marketplace listings (Shopee, Amazon), catalog

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (P1)</summary>

A clean e-commerce product photograph of a 200ml facial moisturizer in a frosted glass jar with a matte white screw-top lid, centered on a pure white background. The jar has a minimalist cream-colored label reading "HYDRA GLOW" in thin sans-serif uppercase, with smaller text beneath reading "Daily Moisturizer • 50ml". Shot at eye level with a very slight three-quarter angle to show depth. Soft diffused studio lighting from above and both sides creating completely even illumination with no harsh shadows — only a subtle contact shadow directly beneath the jar. The glass jar shows slight product visibility (pale cream-colored cream inside). Ultra-clean, sharp focus across entire product, photorealistic rendering, pure white infinity background, commercial catalog photography. No props, no decorations, no text other than on the product label.
</details>

### P2: Luxury Dark
- Black/charcoal background, dramatic directional lighting
- Gold/warm highlights, reflective surface beneath product
- Smoke, subtle lens flare, premium feel
- Best for: watches, perfume, electronics, spirits

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (P2)</summary>

A premium product photograph of a luxury men's chronograph watch on a deep black seamless background. The watch stands upright at a slight three-quarter angle, stainless steel case with black dial, two silver subdials, slim hour markers, and a tachymeter bezel. Black leather strap with red stitching along both edges. Placed on a glossy wet black surface creating mirror-like reflections beneath. Dramatic warm lighting from upper left creating golden highlights on the steel case and crisp specular highlights on the crystal. Wisps of subtle smoke curling from behind. High-contrast, low-key lighting, cinematic mood. Ultra-detailed commercial product photography, shallow depth of field with soft bokeh in far background, realistic metal reflections, premium luxury watch campaign aesthetic. Color palette: black, charcoal, silver steel, warm gold accents. No text, no watermark, no other objects.
</details>

### P3: Lifestyle Flat Lay
- Top-down arrangement with complementary props
- Natural materials (wood, marble, linen) as surface
- Soft overhead light, styled but natural
- Best for: skincare, stationery, food, accessories

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (P3)</summary>

A styled flat lay product photograph shot from directly above on a warm light oak wood surface. In the center, a 100ml frosted glass bottle of facial toner labeled "PUREBLOOM" in minimal gold serif text. Surrounding the bottle in an aesthetically arranged composition: a small ceramic dish with 3 dried lavender sprigs, a folded cream linen napkin in the upper left, 2 cotton pads stacked casually, a thin gold ring as a subtle lifestyle prop, and a small sprig of fresh eucalyptus in the lower right. Soft diffused overhead lighting creating gentle shadows beneath each object. Color palette: warm wood, frosted white, sage green, muted gold, cream. Flat lay composition with balanced negative space between elements. Clean, editorial beauty photography, natural and premium. Square 1:1 format. No watermark, no text other than product label.
</details>

### P4: Ingredient/Context Story
- Product surrounded by raw ingredients or use-context items
- Splash, pour, or explosion effect showing what's inside
- Vibrant, sensory, appetizing
- Best for: food, beverages, skincare with natural ingredients

### P5: Miniature Diorama
- Oversized product with tiny figurines interacting
- Construction, exploration, or playful scenes
- Tilt-shift aesthetic, creative and shareable
- Best for: social media virality, creative brands

### P6: Technical Breakdown
- Product with labeled parts, cross-section, or exploded view
- Clean infographic style, arrows and callouts
- Best for: electronics, tools, complex products

### P7: 3D Mockup 📱
- Design/logo rendered ON a physical object (phone, shirt, mug, box, billboard)
- Realistic material interaction (print wrapping, fabric drape, screen glow)
- Clean environment, focus on the mockup object
- Best for: brand presentations, portfolio showcases, client proposals
- Visual cue: _"Kiểu Behance/Dribbble — design hiển thị trên vật thể 3D"_

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (P7)</summary>

A photorealistic 3D mockup of a minimalist logo (white geometric bird icon) printed on a matte black ceramic coffee mug. The mug is a standard 11oz size with a C-shaped handle on the right, placed on a light oak wood desk. The logo is centered on the front face of the mug, printed in crisp white with slight glossy sheen catching the light. Behind the mug, a blurred laptop and a small potted succulent provide subtle context. Soft natural window light from the left, creating a gentle shadow to the right of the mug. Shallow depth of field, warm neutral tones (charcoal, oak, white, sage green). Clean modern office aesthetic. Commercial product photography style, ultra-detailed ceramic texture, realistic print application following the mug's cylindrical curvature. No watermark, no extra text.
</details>

## 👗 Model + Product

### M1: Editorial Fashion
- Dramatic pose, strong styling, bold location
- Cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field
- Full outfit context, aspirational mood
- Best for: clothing, luxury accessories, fashion brands

### M2: Casual Lifestyle
- Model in everyday setting (street, home, café)
- Natural pose, candid feel, warm tones
- Product worn/used naturally, not overly staged
- Best for: casual wear, daily-use products, Gen Z brands

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (M2)</summary>

A warm editorial-style lifestyle photograph of a young Vietnamese woman in her early 20s sitting at a minimalist café table by the window. She wears an oversized cream linen shirt tucked into high-waisted light blue jeans, with a small tan leather crossbody bag on the table. She holds a ceramic coffee cup with both hands, looking out the window with a natural soft smile. The setting is a cozy modern café with exposed brick wall, green hanging plants, and warm wood furniture. Soft natural window light from the left creating gentle highlights on her face, shallow depth of field blurring the café interior behind her. Subtle film grain, muted warm tones (cream, caramel, sage green), relaxed candid composition. 35mm photography aesthetic, realistic skin texture, natural hair strands. 4:5 portrait format. No watermark, no heavy makeup, no plastic skin. Premium fashion catalog mood, authentic and approachable.
</details>

### M3: Beauty Close-Up
- Face/skin focus, dewy lighting, clean background
- Product placed near model or in-hand
- Flawless but realistic skin texture
- Best for: skincare, makeup, beauty devices

### M4: Active/Sporty
- Motion, energy, outdoor or gym setting
- Dynamic angle, slight motion blur on background
- Bright, high-contrast, energetic
- Best for: sportswear, fitness equipment, supplements

### M5: Studio Portrait + Product
- Clean backdrop (white/color), controlled lighting
- Model holding or wearing product prominently
- Commercial catalog feel, versatile for multiple uses
- Best for: any product needing human scale/context

### M6: Virtual Try-On 👗
- Product (clothing/accessory) shown worn on a model naturally
- Realistic fabric draping, material physics, body interaction
- Can be studio or lifestyle setting
- Best for: fashion e-commerce, accessory showcase, before-purchase visualization
- Visual cue: _"Kiểu Shopee/Zalora — áo quần mặc lên người thật"_

## 🎨 Poster / Social Visual

### S1: Typography-Driven
- Large bold text as primary visual element
- Product/image secondary or integrated into letters
- Strong color blocks, graphic design feel
- Best for: event announcements, quotes, brand statements

<details><summary>📝 Example prompt (S1)</summary>

A bold typographic poster for a Summer Sale event. Vertical 9:16 format. The word "SALE" dominates the composition in massive 3D block letters, each letter a different vibrant color (S=coral red, A=golden yellow, L=electric blue, E=mint green). The letters have realistic depth, subtle shadows, and glossy plastic texture. Between and around the letters, small lifestyle product images are scattered (sunglasses, sandals, a tote bag, a water bottle) as if floating in space. Background is a clean off-white with subtle geometric confetti. Below the main text, smaller clean sans-serif text reads "UP TO 70% OFF • JUNE 1-15" in charcoal gray. At the top, a simple wordmark logo reading "URBANSTORE" in thin uppercase. Modern graphic design aesthetic, playful yet premium, strong visual hierarchy, clean composition. No watermark.
</details>

### S2: City/Travel Style
- Illustrated or photographic landmark scene
- Text overlay with event/brand info
- Artistic composition (S-curve, layered depth)
- Best for: events, tourism, location-based brands

### S3: Double Exposure
- Two images blended (portrait + landscape, product + pattern)
- Dreamy, artistic, conceptual
- Best for: brand storytelling, album covers, artistic content

### S4: Infographic Poster
- Data/steps/features visualized with icons and layout
- Grid-based, clear hierarchy, scannable
- Best for: educational, how-to, feature announcements

### S5: Retro/Vintage
- Aged paper texture, limited color palette, nostalgic typography
- Film grain, halftone, or risograph effect
- Best for: F&B, music, craft brands, throwback campaigns

### S6: Multi-Frame Grid 🖼️
- Multiple panels/shots in a single image (2x2, 3x3, 1x3)
- Consistent style across all panels, clean grid separators
- Each panel shows different angle, scene, or sequence step
- Best for: product angles, TVC storyboard, before/after, catalog overview
- Visual cue: _"Kiểu TVC storyboard hoặc 4-góc sản phẩm trong 1 ảnh"_
